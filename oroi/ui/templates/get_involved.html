Get Involved

declared. is a collaborative effort that relies on grant funding and contracts to be sustainable. There’s lots of ways to get involved; some are outlined here and please get in touch if you’d like to be involved in another way!

declared.'s sustainability is based on making sure that everyone who does work for us gets paid for that work, so we don’t accept volunteer contributions.

Government

declared. provides a way for local and national governments to publish declarations of interest in a consistent manner, reducing scope for misunderstandings in use of the data, reducing FoI costs and encouraging democratic engagement.

If you’d like declared. to include your existing declarations in the database and open data feed, then get in touch.

Journalists & News Organisations

We hope that you find declared. helpful; if you’re using it to research stories, please let us know when you publish; we love to see it used!

We’re exploring options for developing subscription data services on top of the open data set; get in touch if you’d like to hear more about that as the work develops.

If there’s data that you’re keen to see included, then get in touch!

Activists

If declared. data is, or could be useful in your work, then get in touch! We love to hear how our data is being put to use, and collaborating on making it better. We can work on custom data analysis, adding more data to the collection, or help integrate the data with anything you’re already doing.

Developers

declared. is open source software and all of the data is available under an open license! We’d love to see what other people can build on the foundations we’ve laid. Get in touch if you’d like to discuss how we can collaborate.

Grantmakers

There’s lots of potentially impactful ways that declared. data can be used, from holding power to account, to being an input to the digital newsroom, and keeping democracy ticking along. If you make grants in this area, please let us know; we rely on grants to develop the platform!

Individuals

If you’re interested in declared. and would be willing to help cover our costs, get in touch!
